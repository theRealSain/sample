About Eduke

Eduke is an innovative academic assistant designed to simplify the educational journey for students, teachers, and parents alike. By leveraging the power of AI, Eduke provides tools for attendance management, academic performance tracking, and instant support via an AI-driven chatbot. Our goal is to foster an engaging and effective learning environment that evolves alongside the needs of our users. With Eduke, teachers can efficiently manage their classrooms, parents can stay updated on their child’s progress, and students can access tailored guidance to achieve their academic goals. Eduke is more than a tool; it’s a partner in education.
